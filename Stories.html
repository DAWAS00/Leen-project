Voices of Gaza's Children

Real stories of resilience and healing

Sarah's Journey to Peace

Age: 9

"Every time I hear loud sounds, I remember those nights. My mother taught me to breathe slowly and count butterflies in my mind. Now when I feel scared, I think of colorful butterflies flying peacefully."

Coping Strategy:

Visualization and breathing exercises

Ahmad's Safe Space

Age: 11

"I created a special corner in my room with my favorite books and toys. When the sounds become too loud, I go there and read stories. It helps me feel safer and calmer."

Coping Strategy:

Creating a personal safe space

Leila's Art Therapy

Age: 8

"Drawing helps me express my feelings when I'm scared. My counselor taught me to draw my fears and then draw myself being brave. It makes me feel stronger."

Coping Strategy:

Art therapy and emotional expression

Supporting Children Through Trauma

If you know a child experiencing similar challenges, here are ways to help:

  • Create safe spaces for expression
  • Practice calming techniques together
  • Maintain consistent routines
  • Seek professional support when needed
